Intuition-based Bayesian Prior Calculator

Convert your intuitive judgments about proportions into beta distribution parameters for Bayesian analysis of UX research data. This tool helps you quantify your prior knowledge for use in beta-binomial models.

This calculator helps you express your beliefs naturally (as a best guess and uncertainty bounds) and converts them into appropriate α and β parameters for your beta prior.

Use this tool when:

  • You're planning a Bayesian analysis of proportions (such as task completion rates, conversion rates, or agreement percentages)
  • You have intuitions about likely values but need to express them as beta distribution parameters
  • You want to ensure your prior beliefs are properly quantified before collecting new data
